Recipe 3.

Description.

- This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling so it is worth is!

- Prep time roughly 30 minutes. Cooking time 2 hours and 30 minutes. This recipe makes roughly 12 servings.

Ingredients.

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage.
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef.
  • 1/2 cup minced onion.
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed.
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes.
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans tomato sauce.
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste.
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar.
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided.
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves.
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, divided.
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning.
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds.
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper.
  • 12 lasagna sheets.
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese.
  • 1 large egg.
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese , sliced.
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese.

Steps.

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion and garlic in a pan/pot over medium heat until well browned.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Seaon with sugar, 2 tablespoons parslet,basil, 1 teaspoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennal seeds, and pepper. Simmer covered for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  4. Bring a large pot of of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna sheets (or lasagna noodles) in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles (or sheets), and rinse with cold water.
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tablespoons parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  6. Preheat the oven to 190 degrees C.
  7. To assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles (or sheets) lengthwise over meat sauce, overlapping slightly. Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1.2 cups meat sauce over the mozzarella, sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.
  8. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to prevebt sticking either spray foil with cooking spray or make sure the foil doesnt toutch the cheese.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
  10. Let the lasagna rest at room temperature for about 15 minutes before cutting, this helps it to set and firm up.
