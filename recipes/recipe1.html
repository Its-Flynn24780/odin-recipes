Ham and Cheese Puff Pastry Squares.

Description.

- Ham and cheese puff pastry, a simple snack and incredibly easy to make. Their buttery, flakey layers ar wraaped around a savory ham and chees filling.

- Prep time is roughly 15 minutes. Cook time is 20 minutes. This recipe makes 12 severvings.

Ingredients.

  • 1 (2 sheet) pack of frozen puff pastry.
  • 2 tablespoons honey mustard.
  • 1/4 pound thinly sliced deli ham.
  • 1/4 pound sliced Swiss cheese.
  • 2 tablespoons oil-packed sun-dried tomato slivers (optional).
  • 1 large egg.
  • 1 tablespoon of water.
  • 1 teaspoon everthing bagel seasoning.
  • chopped fresh parsley, for garnish.

Steps.

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees C. Line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  2. Place a sheet of puff pastry on a lighly-floured sufrace. Carefully roll into a rectangle about 10x12 inches, gently smoothing out the creases.
  3. Place the pastry sheet on the prepared baking tray, and spread with honey mustard, leaving a 1-inch border around the edge. Evenly place ham slices inside the border, top with swiss cheese and sprinkle sun-dried tomatoes on top.
  4. Whisk egg and water in a small bowl to create an egg wash. lightly brush egg around the edges of the pastry.
  5. Roll out the second sheet of puff pastry to the same size as before (Roughly 10x12 inchs), and place it over the ham-cheese mixture. Line up the edges, press down until both sheets meet, and crimp the edges with a fork. Brush the top with the remaining egg wash, and sprinkle with Everthing bagel seasoning.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until puffed and golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes. Cool for about 5 minutes, then you can cut into 12 equal sqaures with either a sharp knife or a pizza cutter. Garnnish with chopped parsley and serve.
