English Muffin Pizzas.

Description.

- English muffin mini pizzas, a very fast and kid friendly recipe. They can choose their own toppings and put them together, all you have to do is bake them.

- Prep time roughly 10 minutes. Cooking time 10 minutes. This recipe makes 4 servings (of 2 pizzas).

Ingredients.

  • 4 English muffins (split in half).
  • 1/2 cup canned pizza sauce.
  • 2 cups shreeded mozzarella cheese.
  • 16 slices of pepperoni sausage (2 for each one).

Steps.

  1. Once all ingredients have been gathered, Preheat the oven to 190 degrees c.
  2. Arrange the english muffin halves cut-side up on a baking tray.
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly on top of each one.
  4. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese nd top with the pepperoni slices.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and browned on the edges (about 10 minutes).
  6. Now they are done and ready to serve!
